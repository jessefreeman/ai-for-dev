# Grok's Twitter/X Search Specialty

## Context

From [[summary-matthew-berman-ai-models|Matthew Berman's "Every AI Model Explained"]] video. See also [[Grok]].

## Content

Grok (by xAI, Elon Musk) is a capable frontier model with one standout strength: **real-time access to Twitter/X data**.

**What it does**: References live information from Twitter/X at any time. Can research trends, monitor what's being said about any topic, analyze real-time social media sentiment.

**Where to use Grok:**
- Twitter/X trend research
- Real-time social media monitoring
- Topics where the most current information lives on X
- Research into public figures who are active on X

**Where NOT to use Grok** (per Matthew Berman): General work tasks, coding, document analysis, deep research. It doesn't carry its weight against Claude, Gemini, or ChatGPT for these use cases — "it's just not as good as those models" for general purposes.

**Pricing note**: $30/month paid tier is relatively expensive vs feature set compared to alternatives.

**Bottom line**: Use Grok specifically when you need live Twitter/X intelligence. Use any of the other frontier models for everything else.
