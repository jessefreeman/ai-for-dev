# Fit Assessment Tool — Inverting the Hiring Power Dynamic

## Context

From [[summary-nate-b-jones-ai-professional-interface|Nate B Jones's professional interface video]]. See also [[AI Professional Interface]].

## Content

The most novel component: a tool where employers paste a job description and your AI gives an honest assessment of fit.

**Strong fit**: "Let's talk. Here's my platform API experience, cross-functional leadership, comfort with ambiguity." With evidence the employer can verify via the AI chat.

**Weak fit**: "This role needs deep consumer product experience. My career has been B2B. I'm probably not your person. But if you have roles that match, let's talk."

**Why the courage to say no is the power move**:
- Inverts the traditional dynamic: "let's figure out together" vs "please decide if I'm worthy"
- Signals confidence in market position — not desperate for any chance
- Provides genuine utility — saves hiring managers time on mismatches
- The AI runs at zero marginal cost but delivers real value per interaction

**Traditional framing**: Candidate is supplicant. Employer controls gates. Squeeze through their keyhole.
**Interface framing**: Both sides evaluate fit. "My time also has value."

This positioning changes everything about how you're perceived.
