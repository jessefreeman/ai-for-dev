# Database Reactivation ROI Numbers

## Context

From [[summary-nate-herk-500-ai-workflows|500 AI Workflows]] by [[Nate Herk]]. See [[AI Automation Workflows]].

## Content

Database reactivation re-engages forgotten CRM contacts — past customers, cold leads, free trial drop-offs — with personalized outreach referencing their history.

**Why it works**: These people already know the business. Converting a warm-but-forgotten lead is exponentially cheaper than converting a cold stranger. Most businesses sit on this goldmine while spending all their budget on new leads.

**ROI numbers**:
- Agencies specializing in database reactivation report average **1,200% ROI in the first 60 days**
- Even a modest **2–3% conversion** of a cold contact list = significant revenue

**Gym example**: 4,000 contacts, zero outreach. 2–3% reactivation = 80–130 people back through the door. At $50/month × 8-month average retention = **$32,000–$48,000 in recovered revenue with zero new ad spend.**

Best for: high-LTV recurring businesses (gyms, dental, SaaS, e-commerce, coaching). Requires 500+ inactive contacts to be worthwhile.
