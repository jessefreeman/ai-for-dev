# Sell Outcomes Not Automations

## Context

From [[summary-nate-herk-500-ai-workflows|500 AI Workflows]] by [[Nate Herk]]. See [[AI Automation Workflows]].

## Content

**The principle**: Never sell the automation. Sell the outcome the automation creates.

- ❌ "I'll build you an n8n workflow"
- ❌ "I'll set up a Claude Code automation"
- ✅ "I can save you 10 hours a week"
- ✅ "I can cut your admin error rate and speed up your lead response"

When you show a business owner the math — their current close rate vs. projected close rate after automation, or current labor cost vs. after — price objections largely disappear. The expensive decision becomes *not* hiring you.

**Two positioning paths**:
- **Niche specialist**: Master one workflow deeply across many industries. Better case studies, stronger positioning, higher prices. Like a steakhouse that doesn't need hot dogs.
- **Generalist consultant**: Understand all five workflows. Your job is diagnosis, not pitching a pre-built solution. Find the real bottleneck, then apply the right tool.
