# AI Professional Interface — The Five Components

## Context

From [[summary-nate-b-jones-ai-professional-interface|Nate B Jones's professional interface video]]. See also [[AI Professional Interface]].

## Content

Five components that replace the traditional resume/application:

1. **AI Chat ("Ask AI About Me")** — visitors query freely, AI answers from your real work. Multi-turn depth is hard to fake at scale, unlike resume bullet points.

2. **Expandable AI Context** — behind each resume bullet, the full story. "$1.2M cost reduction" → inherited $4M AWS spend → built cost transparency → rightsized instances → lessons learned. Claims become narratives.

3. **Honest Skills Matrix** — three columns: Strong, Moderate, Gaps. Publishing gaps signals self-awareness and confidence. Hiring managers drowning in candidates who claim everything find this refreshing.

4. **Fit Assessment Tool** — paste a job description, AI gives honest assessment. Strong fit: "let's talk + evidence." Weak fit: "I'm not your person, but here's what transfers." Inverts the power dynamic.

5. **Clean Professional Site** — the container. Built in Lovable in 2-3 hours. Functions as conversion optimization (what they find when they arrive), not lead generation.

**Buildable as a client service**: Deep interview → AI training → site build → fit tool config → distribution guidance.
