# The Clogged Pipe Diagnostic Framework

## Context

From [[summary-nate-herk-500-ai-workflows|500 AI Workflows]] by [[Nate Herk]]. See [[AI Automation Workflows]].

## Content

**The framework**: Think of a business as a pipe with revenue flowing through it. Spending more on ads or hiring more salespeople when there's a clog upstream just puts more water into a broken pipe.

**The diagnostic question**: *"If 500 new clients showed up at your business tomorrow, what would break first?"*

This question forces the owner to mentally walk through their entire operation step by step. Every answer reveals a bottleneck — and a potential automation deal. The bottleneck that breaks first is where you start, not where the owner thinks the problem is.

How the five workflows map to common clogs:
| Workflow | Clog |
|---|---|
| Speed to Lead | Response gap |
| Document Processing | Operational bottleneck |
| Follow-up Sequences | Leaky pipeline |
| Database Reactivation | Forgotten revenue |
| Internal Reporting | Visibility gap |

Fix the clog first. Then help the business put more water through the pipe.
