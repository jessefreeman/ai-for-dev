# Gemini's Unique Video Ingestion Capability

## Context

From [[summary-matthew-berman-ai-models|Matthew Berman's "Every AI Model Explained"]] video. See also [[Gemini]].

## Content

As of early 2026, Gemini does something no other frontier model matches: **frame-by-frame video ingestion**.

Upload a video to Gemini. Ask questions about any specific moment in the video. Gemini watches the full video frame by frame and can answer questions about what's happening at any point.

**Use cases**: Research, content analysis, extracting information from recorded meetings, analyzing video footage, understanding visual sequences.

**How this differs from image input**: Other models (Claude, ChatGPT) accept images and can describe what's in them. Gemini processes the temporal sequence of a full video — it can reason about what happened before and after a specific frame, track objects across time, understand narratives.

**Matthew Berman's summary**: "Gemini still does something that no other model does — ingest video." As of the video (March 2026), Claude and ChatGPT had caught up on context window (1M tokens) but not on video ingestion.

**Combined strength**: Gemini's video ingestion + Google Search integration makes it the best tool for deep research tasks that involve video content or require extensive web source synthesis.
