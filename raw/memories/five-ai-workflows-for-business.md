# The 5 AI Automation Workflows Businesses Actually Pay For

## Context

From [[summary-nate-herk-500-ai-workflows|500 AI Workflows]] by [[Nate Herk]]. Full details in [[AI Automation Workflows]].

## Content

Businesses don't want impressive AI — they want simple workflows that save time, save money, or eliminate mistakes. The five highest-value automations:

1. **Speed to Lead** — respond to inbound inquiries within seconds; responding in 5 min = 10x conversion vs 30 min. Average business takes 47 hours.

2. **Document Processing** — extract data from invoices/contracts; often rule-based (no AI needed); saves 45+ hours/week for document-heavy businesses.

3. **Follow-up Sequences** — automated nurture for warm leads; 80% of sales need 5+ follow-ups but most reps stop after 1–2.

4. **Database Reactivation** — re-engage forgotten CRM contacts; 2–3% conversion of a cold list = significant revenue with zero ad spend.

5. **Internal Reporting & Status Notifications** — automated data pull and delivery to where teams already look; stickiest automation (teams can't go back once they have it).

These map to the five most common business bottlenecks: response gap, operational bottleneck, leaky pipeline, forgotten revenue, visibility gap.
