## Key Ideas

- The modern developer is no longer a full-stack implementor but an agent manager — hiring the right agent for each role
- Every vibe-coded app looks the same; specialized design skills ([[Impeccable]]) and context management ([[Open Viking]]) are the differentiators
- You can train your own LLM for ~$100 — not competitive with frontier models but gives you absolute control
- **"When intelligence becomes abundant, software stops charging per human"** — the SaaS death spiral thesis. Per-seat pricing is the structural target, not any single product.
- **"None of these AI companies have much of a moat"** — the real battle is who builds the best platform for autonomous code orchestration
