## Key Ideas

- **AutoResearch generalizes beyond ML**: Apply objective metrics + automated measurement + a variable to any process — skills, websites, cold email, landing pages
- **Binary evals are king**: Yes/no questions give the tightest signal for prompt optimization; Likert scales compound variance
- **The research log is the real asset**: Pass it to a future smarter model to continue where predecessors left off
- Skills are just prompts, and prompts are inherently noisy — run them many times and measure the distribution, not a single output
