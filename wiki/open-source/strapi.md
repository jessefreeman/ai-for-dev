# Strapi

Open-source headless CMS. Free self-hostable for small teams (the paid tier adds SSO and support). Used as the structured-content backbone for blog/newsletter publishing pipelines, especially when paired with a JAMstack frontend.

## Position

[[self-hostable-alternatives|Self-hostable alternative]] to **Contentful / Sanity / WordPress (for headless use) / Notion (CMS portion)**. The third leg of the [[summary-simon-hoiberg-self-hostable-alternatives|Notion replacement split]] alongside [[docmost]] and [[plane]].
