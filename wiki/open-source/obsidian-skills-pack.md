# Obsidian Skills Pack

Claude skill pack that gives the agent **vault-native AI** for [[obsidian|Obsidian]]: auto-tagging, auto-linking, and direct access to the user's notes. **16,000 GitHub stars** at time of source. Per the source: built by the Obsidian creator (Kepano).

> **Verification flag:** the source attributes the pack to "the CEO of Obsidian, Kapono, the creator of Obsidian, not just some random community member." Worth confirming the maintainer attribution against the actual repo before treating it as Obsidian-official rather than community-built. Recorded as the source claim, not as wiki ground truth.

## What it does

- Auto-tags notes
- Auto-links related notes
- Lets Claude read your notes, tag them, link related ideas, and use accumulated knowledge while working on projects
