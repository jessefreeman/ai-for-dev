# Mirrorish

A multi-agent AI prediction engine that simulates a social network of agents with independent personalities reacting to real-world data. Primarily in Chinese.

## How It Works

1. Extracts data from the internet — breaking news, financial trends, macro/micro indicators
2. Creates a digital world where multiple agents with independent personalities discuss and react to the data
3. Agents form a miniature artificial social network that evolves as new data arrives
4. Use the emergent patterns to predict trends, evaluate strategies, or analyze market movements

## Note

The interface and documentation are primarily in Chinese. Fireship's take: "If you don't know how to speak Chinese yet... you're falling behind."
