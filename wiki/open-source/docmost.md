# Docmost

Open-source collaborative wiki and documentation platform. Self-hostable; covers the **docs and notes** half of Notion well without trying to be an everything-app. Easy to deploy to your own server or any cloud platform.

## Position

[[self-hostable-alternatives|Self-hostable alternative]] to **Notion** (docs/wiki portion only). Pairs with [[plane]] (project management) and [[strapi]] (CMS) to fully replace a Notion-everything setup.
