# Marketing Skills Pack (Corey Haines)

Skill pack by Corey Haines covering the marketing layer most vibe coders skip. **16,000 GitHub stars** at time of source — the most popular *non-coding* skills package for [[claude-code|Claude Code]].

## What it includes

20+ sub-skills spanning:

- CRO (conversion rate optimization)
- Copywriting
- SEO
- Email sequences
- Growth engineering

## Type and platform

- **Type:** skill-pack
- **Platforms:** [[claude-code|Claude Code]]
- **Status:** tracked

## Why it matters in the wiki

The wiki tracks the **post-build distribution gap** as an open thread under [[saas-death-spiral]] and [[ai-professional-interface]]: most AI builders ship the product and then their app sits at three downloads. This pack is one of the wiki's first entries on a tooling answer to that gap *that lives inside the same harness as the build itself.*
