# Plane

Open-source project management tool. Minimalist by design — covers the core PM use cases without scope creep. Free self-hostable Community Edition; a paid lifetime upgrade ($799 one-time at the time of [[summary-simon-hoiberg-self-hostable-alternatives|Simon Høiberg's coverage]]) unlocks Cycles, time tracking, and real-time collaboration.

## Position

[[self-hostable-alternatives|Self-hostable alternative]] to **Jira / Linear / Notion (PM portion) / ClickUp**. Part of the three-way Notion split alongside [[docmost]] (wiki) and [[strapi]] (CMS).
