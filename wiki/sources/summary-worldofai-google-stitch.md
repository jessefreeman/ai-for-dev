# Google Stitch: FULLY FREE New AI UI Designer

**Source**: YouTube — [[worldofai|WorldofAI]], published 2025-05-26
**Tools covered**: [[google-stitch|Google Stitch]], [[gemini|Gemini]] 2.5 Pro / Flash

## Summary

WorldofAI walks through **[[google-stitch|Google Stitch]]** at its Google I/O 2025 launch — a free Gemini-powered text-to-UI designer positioned as a v0.dev alternative. The headline detail this source adds beyond [[summary-nate-b-jones-google-stitch|Nate B Jones's coverage]] is the **two-mode pricing structure** (Standard mode runs Gemini 2.5 **Flash** at 350 generations/month, Experimental mode runs Gemini 2.5 **Pro** at 50 generations/month) and the **export options** (Figma-ready files OR static HTML/CSS). Demos: an Airbnb clone generated from a screenshot, and a multi-page Daily Stoic app with light/dark mode and quote history — both produced from single workflows.

## What this source adds

- **Two-mode model split**: Standard (Gemini 2.5 Flash, 350 gen/month) vs Experimental (Gemini 2.5 Pro, 50 gen/month)
- **Export to Figma OR HTML/CSS**: confirms the design.md export Nate B Jones flagged is one of three output formats
- **Visual mockup replication**: paste a screenshot, get a working clone (Airbnb demo)
- **Sketch-to-design**: attach a hand-drawn sketch image, Stitch produces the polished version
- **Theme controls**: colors, corner radius, light/dark appearance

## Sibling Google I/O 2025 launch

This is one of two free tools Google launched at I/O 2025, both Gemini-powered, both covered by WorldofAI in the same week:

- [[jules|Jules]] — async coding agent (Gemini 2.5 Pro)
- **Stitch** — UI designer (Gemini 2.5 Pro / Flash)

Both fit the [[saas-death-spiral|SaaS death spiral]] thread: Google shipping free, capable AI products against entrenched paid incumbents (v0.dev, Figma; Codex, Copilot).
