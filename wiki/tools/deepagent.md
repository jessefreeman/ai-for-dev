# DeepAgent

Abacus AI's general-purpose autonomous agent. Sibling product to **[[codellm|Abacus AI CodeLLM]]** but targeted at **research, dashboards, and multi-step business workflows** rather than coding. Bundled in the **ChatLLM Teams plan (~$10/month)** with **3 free DeepAgent tasks**, then unlimited on upgrade. Native integrations with Gmail, Slack, and Jira.

- **Vendor**: Abacus AI
- **Sibling product**: [[codellm|CodeLLM]] (same plan, same price)
- **Pricing**: ~$10/month bundle (with CodeLLM, ChatLLM, Computer Agent); 3 free DeepAgent tasks at the entry tier
- **Integrations**: Gmail, Slack, Jira

## Capabilities

- **Deep research** — multi-source synthesis with citations
- **Full-stack websites** — Next.js apps from a single prompt
- **Interactive dashboards** — live charts and data tables on demand
- **Multi-step automation** — chained tool calls across the integrated services

Demo outputs from [[summary-worldofai-deepagent|the source]]: a 3-day Bali trip itinerary with a live dashboard, an academic-paper synthesis with citations, and a Next.js dashboard generated from one prompt.
