# Grok

xAI's (Elon Musk) large language model. A capable frontier model with one standout specialty: real-time access to Twitter/X data.

## Strengths

- **Twitter/X search**: Unique ability to reference live information from Twitter/X at any time. Best tool for research involving trends, real-time sentiment, or anything happening on X.
- General reasoning and generation capabilities comparable (though not superior) to other frontier models

## Limitations

Not at feature parity with [[ChatGPT]], [[Claude]], or [[Gemini]] for general use. Not the best choice for most work tasks, coding, or research outside Twitter.

## Tiers (as of early 2026)

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Free | $0 |
| Paid | $30/month |
| Heavy use | $300/month |

## Use Case

Best suited for: Twitter trend research, real-time social media analysis, anything requiring up-to-the-minute X/Twitter data.
