# Gemini

[[google|Google]]'s frontier LLM family. Notable for video ingestion, deep research capabilities, and tight Google ecosystem integration. See [[google]] for the company-level entity (Google DeepMind, Vertex AI, Genkit, Gemma 4, Firebase Studio, Stitch).

## Unique Capabilities

- **Video ingestion**: Upload a video and ask questions about any specific frame. Gemini watches the full video frame-by-frame and can answer questions about specific moments. No other frontier model (Claude, ChatGPT) does this as of early 2026.
- **1M token context window**: One of the first models to reach this scale; Claude and ChatGPT have since caught up.
- **Google integrations**: Best-in-class connections to Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Search. Natural advantage from being Google.
- **NanoBanana image generation**: Google's code name for their image generation model — included in the Gemini interface.

## Strengths

- **Deep research**: Gemini Deep Thinking rated best by Matthew Berman for compiling reports from many sources
- **Speed**: Google's custom TPUs enable fast inference
- **Search**: Best web search of any frontier model (native Google integration)

## Tiers (as of early 2026)

| Plan | Price | Notable |

| Free | $0 | Gemini 3 Flash, limited 3.1 Pro, image gen, deep research, Gemini Live |
| AI Plus | ~$19/month | More usage, video model access |

Free tier is notably generous — includes deep research and image generation.

## Compared to Other Frontier Models

According to [[Matthew Berman]]:
- Gemini: best for **search and deep research**
- [[Claude]]: best for **work and coding**
- [[ChatGPT]]: best for **ease of use**
